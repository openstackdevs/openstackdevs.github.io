# About OpenStack Devs

We are infrastructure engineers focused on building and operating cloud-native platforms at scale.

## What We Cover

- **OpenStack Deployment**: Production deployment patterns, upgrade strategies, and operational best practices
- **Service Development**: Building and extending OpenStack services with custom APIs and integrations
- **Ceph Integration**: Storage architecture, performance tuning, and reliability patterns
- **Magnum & Kubernetes**: Container infrastructure, COE management, and CNCF ecosystem
- **Operations**: Monitoring, troubleshooting, security hardening, and performance optimization

## Our Approach

We believe in production-tested solutions, clear documentation, and sharing knowledge from real-world deployments.
